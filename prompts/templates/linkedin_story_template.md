# LinkedIn Storytelling Template

Generate a high-performing LinkedIn authority-building post.

Goals:
- build credibility
- increase engagement
- increase authority
- increase saves and shares
- generate professional trust

---

# Structure

1. Attention-Grabbing Hook
2. Personal Story or Insight
3. Problem or Challenge
4. Lesson Learned
5. Valuable Takeaway
6. CTA

---

# Hook Examples

Use:
- unexpected realizations
- failures
- lessons
- industry observations
- strong opinions
- emotional experiences

---

# Writing Style

- professional
- authentic
- intelligent
- emotional but concise
- story-driven
- high readability

---

# Optimization Rules

Optimize for:
- retention
- readability
- saves
- shares
- comments

Use:
- short paragraphs
- whitespace
- conversational flow

---

# Emotional Triggers

Use:
- vulnerability
- ambition
- transformation
- struggle
- achievement
- curiosity

---

# Output

Generate:
- headline hook
- complete LinkedIn post
- CTA
- engagement score
- suggested hashtags

---

# Avoid

- robotic AI tone
- excessive emojis
- fake stories
- overused motivational language
- generic advice
